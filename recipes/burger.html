Chicken Burger

Hii Everyone!!

Burger is an American dish that was invented back in 1904 by Louis Lassen.

This dish is eaten almost everywhere in the world dur to its easy recipe and affordability.

It tastes awesome! Filled with a chicken patty and veggies..

This is how you make it:

Ingredients:

For the Chicken Patty:

  • 250g ground chicken(or finely chopped cooked chicken)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp onion(finely chopped)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2tbsp red chili powder or paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Burger:

  • 2 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Cheese slices(optional)
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, or burger sauce

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Patty:

In a bowl, mix chicken, breadcrumbs, egg, onion, garlic and spices.

Shape into burger-sized patties.

Cook in a pan over medium heat with a little oil, 6-7 minutes per side or until

golden and fully cooked.

2. Toast the buns:

Cut the buns in half and lightly toast them on a pan or in a toaster.

3. Assemble Your Burger:

Spread mayo or sauce on the bottom bun.

Add lettuce, chicken patty, tomato, onion, and cheese(if using).

Top it with the upper bun!

Serve & Enjoy:

Serve hot with fries, ketchup, or a chilled drink!
